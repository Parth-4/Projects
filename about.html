The Story Of CakeLand

After being introduced in 2011, Mary and Megan came up with the dream of Emporium Pies. Before the year had come to a close, their dream was rapidly beginning to take shape in the form of selling pies outside of an old house in the Bishop Arts District of Oak Cliff. With a little help from talented friends along the way, a supportive community surrounding each store, and an endless supply of hungry and happy customers, Emporium Pies has grown up a lot. However, it always has been and always will be about one thing: fine pies for fine folk.
